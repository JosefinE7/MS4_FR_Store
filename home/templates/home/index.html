About
FutureRetro

The FutureRetro is a clothing company created by Erica Johnsson, the FutureRetro customer is a modern-day woman who wants the classic and feminine silhouette of the 1940s-50s while remaining modern feel. The FutureRetro girl tends to dress a little bit conservatively, but the different items can be integrated into every girl's closet!

Every single item created is a one-of-a-kind, ’40-’50s inspired piece that is designed and hand-sewn by me, with fabrics and supplies from three local second-hand stores: Myrorna, Frälsningsarmén och Blocket. It is my own interpretation of the timeless femininity of this era and is intended for women in all walks of life: from the businesswoman to the stay-at-home mom. Every woman deserves to feel feminine and beautiful! For more information about FutureRetro click here!

Love, Erica
