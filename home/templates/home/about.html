The FutureRetro is a clothing company created by Erica Johnsson, the FutureRetro customer is a modern-day woman who wants the classic and feminine silhouette of the 1940s-50s while remaining modern feel. The FutureRetro girl tends to dress a little bit conservatively, but the different items can be integrated into every girl's closet!

Every single item created is a one-of-a-kind, ’40-’50s inspired piece that is designed and hand-sewn by me, with fabrics and supplies from three local second-hand stores: Myrorna, Frälsningsarmén och Blocket. It is my own interpretation of the timeless femininity of this era and is intended for women in all walks of life: from the businesswoman to the stay-at-home mom. Every woman deserves to feel feminine and beautiful!

Love, Erica

FutureRetro is excited to start upcycling. Upcycling is an approach to design where you transform by-products, waste or disused materials or products into something new of better quality and greater value. By reusing materials that already exist, you are saving energy, water, chemicals and other resources required to make new virgin materials.

This may seem like a common sense approach to design but even the smallest design choices can impact how long a product’s life will last. There are many reasons that people discard clothing – not the right fit, the person changes size, the fabric quality or the style becomes uncool. The best way you can keep your products from going to landfill is by designing to last, to be cared for and repaired, to be reused or to be passed on to others. This is our moto here at FutureRetro!
